About me

- Born and raised in San Francisco
- SFSU Graduate w/ 3.81 GPA
- B.S. in Computer Science

Computer science got me during my first year of college. It was a little program written in Java that created "staircases" of differing heights based on user input. I loved the idea of being able to create something that will act accordingly to the user. Wait, isn't this what games are at the core? ...Yeah, I'm definitely becoming a software developer.

I'm an avid gamer who likes to spend his free time having fun with friends. Games have taught me how to be the most efficient with my resources, which is vital to software developers. Plus, they inspire me to keep going and be my best. I enjoy all sorts of games, except scary games. And MOBAs.
